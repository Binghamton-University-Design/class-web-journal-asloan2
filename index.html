Project 1: Camera of the Future

I found inspriation for my design from the Camera C3 Argus. I wanted my design for the Camera of the Future to be idealized from the perspective of the writer in the article. It should have whimsy, but also have imperfections in a charming way.

The camera is the size of a walnut, so I compared it to a walnut in this sketch.

I have taken away the chord that extends down the sleeve, but I still wanted the camera to wrap around the head of the wearer. The final design is on the right side of the page.

Project 1: Camera of the Future

A magazine ad for the Camera of the Future. I imitated the romantic style of 1940's and 1950's advertisements and replaced the image of a regular consumer and home-owner with a scientist. The product is enlarged on the side to show what it looks like up-close and displayed on the wearer on the right.

This is the complete design of the Camera of the Future. With this view, you can see all of the details that you would not be able to see if the camera was its true size.
